More exciting news on the festival front, with a couple of sets booked in to the [Three Rivers Music Festival](https://threeriversfest.uk/), at Porchfield, over the weekend of 29-31st of May.

On the Community Stage, we have a full *Monty + The AnyWhens* set on the Community Stage. This is the first time we'll be performing to an essentially open crowd, so it'd be great to see some regular faces in the mix, as well as a hopefully whole new audience to try to convert to the magic of story. A little earlier in the day, I've also got a slot on the Woodland Stage as part of Tim / King Stammers spoken word takeover. For that set, I'll be joining with Sophie Kate as she brings some of her fiddle magic along to liven up the stories.

We've not been around for this festival in previous years, but with the likes of [Dub Pistols](https://dubpistolsmusic.co.uk/) and [Undercover Hippy](https://undercoverhippy.com/) heading the bill, both of whom we tend to catch whenever the opportunity presents itself, it should be a great weekend.

__[Three Rivers Music Festival](https://threeriversfest.uk/), Porchfield. On stage Sunday, 31st May, sometime 11-2 on the Woodland Stage with Sophie KAte on Fiddle, and then + The AnyWhens at 3.30 on the Community Stage.__
