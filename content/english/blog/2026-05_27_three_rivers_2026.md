Just back from five days at [Bearded Theory](https://beardedtheory.co.uk/), where I told a couple of pop up tales round the fire and in [The Magic Teapot](https://www.magicteapot.co.uk/), and now I'm ready to get back in a tent this weekend at the Island's own [Three Rivers Music Festival](https://threeriversfest.uk).

I'll also be tellin' tales a couple of times as part of the bill on Sunday morning, (May 31st, 2026). The first set is from 11.45am-12.15pm, as part of [King Stammers'](https://www.instagram.com/kingstammers/) takeover of the Woodland stage for a couple of Scottish gypsy tales accompanied by Sophie on fiddle, and then again on the Community Stage alongside *The AnyWhens* from half three till half-four.

If there are chances to tell pop-up style round the site before then, I'll maybe pick up on those too...

[*Tickets available here*](https://threeriversfest.uk/tickets/)
